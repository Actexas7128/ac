Privacy Policy

Air Conditioning Texas respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, and service address when you submit a form, request a quote, or contact us through our website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide estimates and service information
  • Schedule appointments
  • Send project updates
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Improve our customer experience

SMS Communication

By submitting your information through our website forms, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Air Conditioning Texas regarding estimates, appointments, service updates, and customer support.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase or service. We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in data with third parties or affiliates.

Information Sharing

Air Conditioning Texas does not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including mobile phone information, with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. We do not use tracking technologies to collect personal information for SMS marketing purposes.
