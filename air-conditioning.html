Signs You Need AC Repair

  • Weak or warm airflow coming from the vents
  • Strange noises or odors when the system runs
  • Rising energy bills with no clear explanation
  • The system runs constantly but never quite cools the house
  • Ice building up on the refrigerant lines or outdoor unit
